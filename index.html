Gem Hunter

Instructions

Your Goal is to reach the random number generated at the start of the game.

Each of the crystals below has a random value assigned to it and every time you click a gem it is added to your total.

You win the game by matching your total score to the goal number. If you bust you lose.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you, and random each game. Good Luck!!
